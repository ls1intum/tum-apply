# AI Extraction

Completing an application form often means re-entering information that already appears in your
documents. To reduce this effort, TUMApply can read your CV and degree certificates and fill in
the corresponding form fields automatically. You then review the suggested values, adjust anything
that is incorrect, and continue.

AI Extraction is available in two places while [applying for a position](./apply):

- **Personal Information** — extract your contact and personal details from your **CV**.
- **Education** — extract your degree details from your **Bachelor's and Master's certificates**.

:::info[Your details remain editable]
Extracted values are placed into ordinary form fields, exactly as if you had entered them
yourself. Nothing is submitted automatically, and you can change any value before continuing.
:::

---

## 🔎 How It Works

1. **Upload the relevant document** as a PDF. The **AI Extraction** button remains disabled until a
file has been uploaded.
2. Select **✨ AI Extraction**. While your document is being processed, the button displays
*"Extracting..."*.
3. The extracted values appear in the corresponding form fields. **Review them for completeness and
accuracy**.

:::note
AI Extraction only fills fields that are **still empty**. It never overwrites information you have
already entered.
:::

## 👤 Personal Information — Extract from your CV

<Image src={cvExtraction} alt="AI Extraction from CV on the Personal Information step" size={ImageSize.large} caption="Personal Information: Upload your CV, then use AI Extraction to fill in your details"/>

On the **Personal Information** step, upload your **Curriculum Vitae** and select **AI Extraction**.

The following details may be filled in automatically:

- **First name** and **last name**
- **Phone number**
- **Website** and **LinkedIn** profile
- **Gender** and **Nationality**
- **Country** and **address** (street, city, postal code)
- **Date of birth**

Your **email address** is taken from your account and cannot be changed here.

---

## 🎓 Education — Extract from your Certificates

<Image src={certificateExtraction} alt="AI Extraction from certificates on the Education step" size={ImageSize.large} caption="Education: upload your Bachelor's and Master's certificates, then use AI Extraction to fill in your degree details"/>

On the **Education** step, upload your **Bachelor's** and/or **Master's certificate** (or
transcript) and select **AI Extraction**.

For each degree, the following details may be filled in automatically:

- **Study program** (for example, *B.Sc. Physics* or *M.Sc. Bioinformatics*)
- **University**
- **Final grade**

:::tip
Grading scales vary between universities. After extraction, please verify the **Final Grade** and
use **Change Scale** if your certificate uses a different scale than the one displayed.
:::

---

## ✅ Important Notes

- **Supported format**: Only PDF files are accepted, up to **25 MB** each.
- **Best results**: Please upload a clear, readable PDF. Scanned or low-quality documents may lead
to incomplete results.
- **Empty fields only**: Information you have already entered is never overwritten.
- **Please review carefully**: Automated extraction may contain errors. Verify every value for
completeness and accuracy before continuing; you remain responsible for the information you
submit.
- **If extraction is unsuccessful**: An *"Extraction Failed"* message appears. Please ensure the
file is a clear, readable PDF, or enter the details manually.

:::warning
AI Extraction is intended to reduce manual typing; it does not replace your own review. Please
verify all extracted details before submitting your application.
:::
