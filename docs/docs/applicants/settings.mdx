# Settings

The **Settings** area is the central place for managing your applicant profile in TUMApply. It is organized as a tab-based interface so that account preferences, notifications, and reusable application data are separated clearly.

For applicants, the available tabs are:

- **General**
- **Notifications**
- **Application Information**
- **Qualifications**

## General

The **General** tab contains your account-level preferences and profile options.

<Image src={settingsGeneralTabImg} alt="General settings tab for applicants" size={ImageSize.large} caption="General settings tab for applicants" />

### Profile Picture

You can personalize your account by adding a profile picture.

<VideoEmbed
  src="https://live.rbg.tum.de/w/artemisintro/74606"
  title="Changing the profile picture in TUMApply"
  caption="Uploading, cropping, and updating the profile picture"
/>

- Click your profile picture area or the edit icon to upload a new image
- Supported file types are **JPEG**, **JPG**, and **PNG**
- The maximum file size is **5 MB**
- After selecting a file, you can crop it before saving
- You can remove your current picture again at any time

<Image src={settingsProfilePictureCropDialogImg} alt="Profile picture crop dialog" size={ImageSize.medium} caption="Uploading and cropping a profile picture" />

Your profile picture is then used anywhere TUMApply shows your user avatar.

### Other General Settings

The **General** tab also includes:

- **Passkeys** for faster and more secure sign-in
- **Appearance** settings for choosing a theme
- **AI Features** consent settings for applicant-side AI support

## Notifications

The **Notifications** tab controls which emails you want to receive from TUMApply.

### Application Emails

Applicants can manage email notifications for:

- **Application Submission** emails when an application is submitted or withdrawn
- **Application Outcome** emails when an application is accepted or rejected

### Subject Area Job Alerts

You can also subscribe to emails about newly published job postings in selected subject areas.

<VideoEmbed
  src="https://live.rbg.tum.de/w/artemisintro/74605"
  title="Managing subject area notifications in TUMApply"
  caption="Managing subject area subscriptions for job posting notifications"
/>

- Enable the subject area notification toggle
- Add one or more subject areas to your selection
- Remove subject areas again whenever your interests change

<Image src={settingsNotificationsSubjectAreasImg} alt="Notification settings with subject area subscriptions" size={ImageSize.large} caption="Subject area notification preferences" />

This is useful if you want to monitor new openings without checking the job overview manually every day.

## Application Information

The **Application Information** tab stores reusable personal information for future applications.

This includes:

- Name and contact details
- Voluntary personal information such as nationality, gender, and date of birth
- Website and LinkedIn profile
- Address information
- Your stored **CV**

<Image src={settingsApplicationInformationImg1} alt="Application information settings overview" size={ImageSize.large} />
<Image src={settingsApplicationInformationImg2} alt="Application information settings details" size={ImageSize.large} caption="Reusable application information for future applications" />

Changes are saved automatically and are intended to prefill future application forms, which reduces repeated data entry.

:::note
Changes in **Settings** affect your applicant profile and are used for future applications. Applications that have already been submitted are not updated retroactively.
:::

### CV Upload and AI Extraction

You can upload your CV directly in this tab. If AI features are enabled for your account, TUMApply can extract information from the CV and use it to help fill empty fields in your stored applicant profile.

## Qualifications

The **Qualifications** tab stores reusable education-related data and supporting documents.

This includes:

- Bachelor degree information
- Master degree information
- Grades and grading-scale related data
- Transcript or degree documents
- Additional supporting documents such as references

<Image src={settingsQualificationsImg1} alt="Qualifications settings overview" size={ImageSize.large} />
<Image src={settingsQualificationsImg2} alt="Qualifications settings details" size={ImageSize.large} caption="Stored qualifications and supporting documents" />

Like the **Application Information** tab, this data is meant to support future applications by keeping your qualifications ready in advance.

## Recommended Workflow

For the smoothest application process, it helps to complete your settings before starting a new application:

1. Add a profile picture if you want a personalized avatar
2. Configure your email and subject-area notifications
3. Fill in your **Application Information**
4. Upload your CV and qualification documents

Once this is done, applying for positions becomes faster because important information is already available in your applicant profile.
